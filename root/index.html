Inide web?

The inide web is often also called "the persoanl web", "the old web" and so much more. And it is a community that is built on personal websites instead of large social platforms. By hosting your own space and maneging your own data you ensure no company can sell or delete your data while also having more control.

Here are some sites if you are intrested in what it is and if it is for you:

  • What is it? indieweb.guide
  • See examples! melonland.net/surf-club
  • Host your own! neocities.org
