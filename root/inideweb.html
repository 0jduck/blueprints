Inide web!

The inide web is often also called "the persoanl web", "the old web" and so much more. And it is a community that is built on personal websites instead of large company owned websites. By hosting your own space and maneging your own data you ensure no company can sell or delete your data making you have more control over your data and yourself as a person.

Make your site

Please be smart about this and the following. I always recomend you do your research and use multiple sources.

To make your own site is suprisingly easy, it can be done quickly and even free. A website is in a simple way file(s) that your browser renders and anyone can for free make their own file(s).

While AI can be easy and quick it is often looked down upon and often comes with many downsides.

Host your site

But you then need to host your website, that part can be harder since it can cost money and can put your personal info at danger if done badly. But as luck has it there are still ways for you to host your own website for free and while being safe.

While you can run your own website without a third party there are sites like neocities.org or for you more tech literate cloudflare.com to name two.
